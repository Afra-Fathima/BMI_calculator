BMI - Body Mass Index

Understanding BMI

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure that uses your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy. It is defined as the individual's body mass divided by the square of their height.

Formula: BMI = weight (kg) / (height (m) * height (m))

BMI Calculator

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater

Why BMI is Important?

BMI is a useful indicator of whether you're a healthy weight for your height. Being overweight or underweight can lead to health issues, so maintaining a healthy BMI can help prevent health problems.

Limitations of BMI:

While BMI is a useful measurement for most people, it does not take into account muscle mass, bone density, overall body composition, and differences related to race and sex. Thus, it may not be accurate for everyone.

Healthy Living Tips

Nutrition

Maintaining a balanced diet is crucial for good health. Ensure you consume a variety of foods, control portion sizes, and include essential nutrients in your diet.

Exercise

Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and reduces the risk of developing various health conditions. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.

Lifestyle

Adopting a healthy lifestyle includes getting enough sleep, managing stress effectively, and having regular health check-ups. These habits contribute to overall well-being.
